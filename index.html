Welcome to KANEPACKAGE PHILIPPINE INC.

KANEPACKAGE PHILIPPINE INC. (KPPI) is committed to the concept of giving all customers Total Satisfaction.

We aim to lead the 21st Century as Global packaging company through developing a highly productive and competent workforce.

About us

Our Objectives

Kanepackage Philippine Inc. (KPPI), a company engaged in designing, manufacturing and sales of various packaging, cushioning and shipping materials, is dedicated to continually improve our performance and ensure the effectiveness of management systems, as well as to provide Total Satisfaction to all relevant interested parties.

Our Mission

We will nurture an environment that stimulates progressively higher standards of individual and team performance and promote harmonious integration of corporate functions. Total Customer Satisfaction constantly challenges the creation capabilities of well-trained and empowered employees who take pride in the work and who share a common desire for constant improvement in pursuit of the company's commitment to full customer's delight.

Our Vision

We deliver “Surprise”, “Good Impression”, and “Comfort” to as many countries/customers as possible speedy and timely through our package design, packages and services. Also, we will aim to be an excellent company by strengthening the trust of stake holders and corporate value.
